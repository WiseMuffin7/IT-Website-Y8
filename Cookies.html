Time for cookies! The choclatey-chip cookies are an easy crumbly snack for the whole family!

Ingredients:

  • 125g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1¾ cups plain flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. In a bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and egg, and beat until well combined.
  4. Sift in the flour, baking soda, and salt, and mix until a dough forms.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions onto the baking tray, leaving room for spreading.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Cool on a wire rack.

Tip: Store cookies in an airtight container for up to a week!
