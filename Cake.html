Need to prep a quick dessert for a celebration, our vanilla cake recipe has got you covered; perfect for any birthday or anniversary!

Ingredients:

  • 125g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ cups self-raising flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a 20cm round cake tin.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a bowl until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Sift in the self-raising flour and salt, and mix until combined.
  5. Stir in the milk and vanilla extract until the batter is smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared tin and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes before turning onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Tip: Decorate with frosting or serve plain with a dusting of powdered sugar!
