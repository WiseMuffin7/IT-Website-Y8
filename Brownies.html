We want you to have the fudgiest choclatey brownies out there! so we've created this ultimate recipe dedicated to Zaven The Great!

Ingredients:

  • 125g unsalted butter, chopped
  • 125g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3 eggs, lightly whisked
  • 1½ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup plain flour
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 20cm square cake pan and line it with baking paper.
  2. In a heatproof bowl over simmering water, melt the butter and dark chocolate together, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat.
  3. Quickly stir in the eggs, sugar, flour, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until just combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  5. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting into squares.

Tip: This recipe yields approximately 16 delicious brownies!
